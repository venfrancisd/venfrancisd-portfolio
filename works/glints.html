Glints

Glints is an HR-Tech startup based in Singapore.

I joined the team of less that 15 in mid-2017 as the only product designer.

Brand Design

I helped created their branding using branding activities such as CORE and Google Venture's Brand Jam.

Building the Glints design foundation

One initiative under my leadership is the Glints Design System. Named after the brightest star in the Earth’s night sky, Sirius is the leading light that guides for the consistent and harmonious product building experience.

Sirius houses guidelines, patterns, reusable user interface components, templates, and other resources to help you create a product for Glints users. Like any other tool, it will only work right when used right. And you can only determine it through a process of constant improvement and practice.
